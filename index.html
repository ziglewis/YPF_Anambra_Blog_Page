What is it about words that make them so endearing?

Just like seeds sown in the ground, they grow when we ponder on them. From thoughts, they form our beliefs and from belief they fuel our actions and what we do overtime become our habit.

Words, however minute or inconsequential they might seem has a ripple effect when acted on. We cannot underestimate the power of words. No wonder when God created the earth, He spoke and things that are not became real; "Let there be" and "there was". God created man in His own image and gave man the same creative ability that He has as a creator. Hence, whatever name Adam calls the animals, that became their name till date.

As professionals, we must be mindful of what we say, how we say it, when we say it, where we say it and for what purpose we say it. "Out of the abundance of the heart, the mouth speaks" (Luke 6:45).

Keep your heart, mouth and ears in check. For what is in your heart (good or bad) that is what you speak and that can either be a blessing or a curse to others that hear you.

You must also be mindful of what you hear. What type of messages do you listen to? What type of songs are on your playlist? Who do you submit yourself to listen to their instructions? All these has an invaluable impact as to how we lead our lives.

In your down moments, when you feel you are under performing and life seems to be at its lowest ebb, what do you do?

Through words, your spirit can be rejuvenated, your strength renewed and your mind resurged. Words of motivation, words of affirmation, words of inspiration and most importantly, the Word of God. Always get a daily dose of these and your life would keep transcending to higher altitudes.

Only after being burned a few times can we understand that fire is always hot. You may not remember the exact end date of the Civil War, but odds are you still know what 8 x 9 is because you had to repeat your multiplication tables over and over, drilling it into your consciousness.

You don't need to thread on a bad road to tell it is bad, you see it and you know; so, reshuffle your words before you dish them out and not vice versa.

In your tongue lies the power of life and death, therefore THINK BEFORE YOU SPEAK.
