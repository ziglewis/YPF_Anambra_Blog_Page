Dear Young Professional,

James Pierce said, "The mind reflects the world, and the world reflects the mind.‚Äù

That means if you want your world, work, home, business or your life, to be productive, peaceful, and organized, it starts from your mind. If you see lot's of chaos and procrastination in your world, you can as well trace it to your mind.

You need to face each new second, minute, hour, day, and week with a decluttered mind. A decluttered mind helps you FOCUS. Focus is key to living a successful life.

How to declutter your mind

  1. Get some sleep.
  2. Have time to meditate.
  3. Transfer your thoughts to paper.
  4. Journaling: list your thoughts and feelings
  5. Set and complete Priorities.
  6. Reduce multitasking
  7. Practice being decisive
  8. Challenge negativity.
  9. Have time for Exercise.
  10. Confide in a loved one.
  11. Enjoy time in nature.
  12. Limit the time you spend on social media.
  13. Take a break either for vacation or just to unwind.

Therefore, declutter your MIND and focus on the most important thing at every point in time and your life will experience significant progress.
