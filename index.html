Dear Young Professional,

Surely, there is an undeniable wisdom a man who has gone through thick and thin in life possesses. Any attempt to disacknowledge it invariably shuts you out from receiving from such man. He has fought a lot of life's battles, difficulties and challenges and has come out victorious. When you discuss with them for a few minutes you would be amazed at the way they speak.

They can speak with so much accuracy and precision as to draw out questions yet unspoken in your mind and proffer solutions to what troubles you. The reason is because your challenge is within the confines of their experience and they can draw from that treasury to make sure they satisfy your longing for an answer. They give refreshments to famished souls on life's road. These are men who would never run from any challenge. They know that when any physical difficulty or mountain is mastered and conquered, an intrinsic value or worth is accrued, this to them is worth fighting for.

Consistency on this path has made them a depository and repository of wisdom. Little wonder, true men glory in their infirmities. To them there is a power that rests on them by reason of every difficulty that is conquered and that power without fail, comes with wisdom to be wielded accurately. They give life to the statement, "counsel in the heart of a man is like deep waters, and only a man of understanding can draw it out". They are often too relevant in whatever sphere of life they find themselves because of the abundance of their experiences.

By reason of having dwelt long on this path of life, they have such a great level of emotional stability that they can absorb whatever comes their way and still smile, not because they are irresponsible, but because they have in their treasury the armoury to fight and conquer. So, they rejoice in advance. All these did not happen in one day, it took time to be built. Any man who must be relevant in his world must allow his life capture the experiences of those who would be under him.

If you are living for yourself alone, you may wish to avoid challenges, if you are for a family, you may wish to scarcely face them, but if you are for a nation or a generation, and you run from them, the gap in experience and wisdom will be visible when you get there, and you would be thrown out of the way. But when you face your challenges and beat them small the deliverance of your world would be apportioned to you and you would become a blessing.

This is the true beauty of "A WISE SOUL".

Humanitarianism is an overflow of a strong sense of spirituality. Spirituality in turn is a sense of meaning, purpose, hope and faith. It is also an individual's understanding of, expression with and connection to that which transcends self. It is related to a deeply held set of personal values and meaning that extends beyond us as individuals.

Only after being burned a few times can we understand that fire is always hot. You may not remember the exact end date of the Civil War, but odds are you still know what 8 x 9 is because you had to repeat your multiplication tables over and over, drilling it into your consciousness.

You don't need to thread on a bad road to tell it is bad, you see it and you know; so, reshuffle your words before you dish them out and not vice versa.
